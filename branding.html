The Cesium Insignia

This is my logo and I use it to establish my brand image, such as accounts, services, and media. It was created by me for me.

The Cesium Cloud

The Cesium Cloud is a privacy-focused Nextcloud instance that I run from my home. It includes file storage, messaging, calling, office, and much more.

Wormhole VPN

Wormhole VPN is a VPN service (obviously) with a built-in ad and tracker blocker. It uses Pi-hole through an OpenVPN connection to deliver an ad-free experience anywhere.

Cesium Industries

Cesium Industries is the fictitious company behind all cesium services on SMPs. It is known for being the first company established on Horizons and owned the Cesium Campus, a few outposts, and Food Llama. It is also considered to own all claimed property on Total Failure.

Semblance

Semblance is a Minecraft mod for the Fabric and Quilt modloader. Semblance allows server admninistrators to change the server branding of their server to hide the server's true brand or for run.

Try Semblance for yourself

Semblance is owned and maintained by EcoBuilder13. I designed the Semblance branding and do not claim ownership over the Semblance project.

Minecraft Horizons

Minecraft Horizons is a modded Minecraft SMP similar to the Hermitcraft server. It started in October of 2020 and features multiple indie content creators.

Learn more about Horizons

Total Failure

Total Failure is a modded hardcore Minecraft series by CStafford14. It premiered in Early 2021 for Season 1, lasting 5 episodes. Season 2 continues.
