Welcome to the Cesium! This site has various bits and pieces from all over the internet. The following is some important information regarding some of the new content.

Images

Social icons on the socials page are from Icons8 and are from the fluency style.

The Guilded icon is from Guilded's own website, and of course, I made the Horizons logo.


The small star on the brands page (which denotes certain graphics) and the chevron on the scroll-to-top button are from Bootstrap Icons.


The non-static backgrounds and graphical transitions are from Haikei.

Fonts

Fonts are from Google Fonts. I use Righteous for branding and big headers, Poppins for subtle headers, and Roboto for body text.

Technical

This site is designed with Bootstrap. This site (as of writing this page) is hosted on GitHub and is open-source. Publishing is done with Netlify, and is served to you via Cloudflare.

The domain is from Freenom and is, of course, free.



Thank you to all who made the images, fonts, and backend. Your services are great!
