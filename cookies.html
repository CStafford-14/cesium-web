Cookies are important to the user experience. They allow site data to be stored on the computer, enabling quicker loading times and allowing the site to remember who you are.

This site uses cookies from CloudFlare to store parts of the site on your device. This cookie will never store any user data and cannot be used for malicious purposes. The comments section on the oppression project is hosted by a 3rd party service, WidgetPack. As it allows for the user to comment with a name, the widget creates a cookie with your information. This is only used to identify you and make sure that you are signed into the widget each time you access the comments section. Again, this cookie cannot be used for malicious purposes. Until you use the comments section, this cookie will never be loaded onto your computer.
